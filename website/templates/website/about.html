About Us

Welcome to AiPrizm Technologies®, your number one source for data mining, analytics, AI and automation. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of services, with a focus on sustainable development and maintenance of predictive and statistical analytics software, data mining, data cleaning and ingestion pipelines along with AI/ML, DevOps and Automation solutions. Founded in 2022 by Siddhesh Pisal, AiPrizm Technologies® has come a long way from its beginnings in Mumbai, India. When Siddhesh Pisal first started out, his passion for finding anomalies, patterns and correlations within large data sets to predict outcomes drove him to create a team of talented data scientists and engineers so that AiPrizm Technologies can offer you modern data analytics solutions for growing your business. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
